How to reach us

By public transportation
  • S-Bahn: Station Tiergarten (S3, S5, S7, S9) + 600m walk (5 min)
  • U-Bahn: Station Ernst-Reuter-Platz (U2) + 500m walk (5 min)
  • Bus: Ernst-Reuter-Platz, Lines M45 and 245 + 500m walk (5 min)
From the airport

Flughafen Berlin Brandenburg (BER):

  • short walk (10 min) to railway station Flughafen BER, take Regional-Express service route RE7 (Direction: Dessau) to S+U Bhf Berlin Zoologischer Garten (30 min) and U2 (Direction Ruhleben) to station U-Bahn: Ernst-Reuter-Platz (2 min) + 500m walk (5 min) or
  • S-Bahn S9 (Direction: Spandau) to S-Bahn: Station Tiergarten (50 min) + 600m walk (5 min)
From the train stations

Berlin Hauptbahnhof (Berlin Central Railway Station):

  • take S-Bahn S3, S5, S7, S9 (Direction: Berlin Zoologischer Garten) to S-Bahn Station Tiergarten (5 min) + 600m walk (5min)
By car

From outside Berlin:

  • Leave the motorway A100 exit Spandauer Damm (Direction City Center) to Ernst-Reuter-Platz -> take the fourth exit Marchstr -> follow this street and turn right into Einsteinufer or
  • Take the motorway A100 to exit Kaiserdamm -> follow the Kaiserdamm/Bismarckstr. to Ernst-Reuter-Platz -> take the fourth exit Marchstr. -> follow this street and turn right into Einsteinufer

From the city center Berlin Mitte:

  • follow Straße des 17. Juni to destination Ernst-Reuter-Platz, behind Charlottenburger Tor turn right in the Einsteinufer
Further Information: Address to our location for GPS Systems: 10587 Berlin, Einsteinufer 17.
